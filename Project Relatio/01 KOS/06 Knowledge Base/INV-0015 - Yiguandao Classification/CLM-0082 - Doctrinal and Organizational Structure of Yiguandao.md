# CLM-0082

# Yiguandao's Doctrine Is a Deliberate Multi-Tradition Synthesis Organized Around the Eternal Mother Cosmology and Three-Age Eschatology, with Confucian Moral Self-Cultivation as Its Dominant Public Strand — a Composition Scholarship Describes as Syncretic-Sectarian, Not as the Doctrine of a Daoist School

## Draft Claim Record

---

# Claim
> Yiguandao's doctrinal architecture centers on the **Eternal/Ancient Mother (Wusheng Laomu) cosmology** and a **three-age eschatology** (the "Green Yang, Red Yang, and White Yang" ages), within which it deliberately incorporates figures and teachings of multiple traditions — Buddhas (Lamplighter, Śākyamuni, Maitreya), "all deities and the various founders of all religions, such as Jesus and Mohammed," Confucian ethics, and Daoist cosmological vocabulary — practiced through initiation ("obtain first, then cultivate"), congregational Buddha-halls, spirit-writing revelation in the Taiwanese sectarian style, and moral self-cultivation; in its contemporary public identity the **Confucian strand is dominant** (Billioud). Scholarship describes this composition as **syncretic-sectarian** — the inherited architecture of the Eternal-Mother sect family — not as the doctrinal system of a school of Daoism.

# Claim Type (KOS-0003 §3)
**Descriptive** — a composition-and-structure claim about a living movement's doctrine and organization as the scholarly literature characterizes it.

---

# Element (a) — Source & Base
- **Strongest available sourcing:** SRC-0134 (live-read) carries the doctrinal specifics quoted above (Mother cosmology; three ages named; the multi-tradition pantheon including Jesus and Mohammed; obtain-then-cultivate soteriology). SRC-0131 (Billioud, current OUP monograph) carries the Confucian-dominance datum at publisher-description level (Confucianism as the movement's public face and authorities-facing strategy). SRC-0133 (Jordan & Overmyer) documents the spirit-writing sectarian practice family at review-verified level; SRC-0132 adds the congregational/organizational analysis at review-verified level.
- **Language & accessibility:** all English; SRC-0134 open (content-verified); SRC-0131/0132/0133 paywalled (bibliographic verification; interiors unread — disclosed).
- **What is and is not establishable:** the composition's main elements and the Confucian public dominance are establishable at the stated verification levels; **precise strand *proportions* (how much of the ritual corpus is Daoist-derived vs. Confucian vs. Buddhist) are NOT establishable from the accessible base** — no source read this session quantifies them — and are recorded as a gap, not estimated.

# Element (b) — Classification Bearing
- **What this claim establishes:** the doctrinal-composition premise of the field's classification: a deliberately **multi-tradition** synthesis under a sect-family cosmology (Eternal Mother, three ages) is the textbook doctrinal profile of the salvationist/sectarian family, and is structurally unlike the doctrinal organization of a Daoist school (which transmits a tradition-specific corpus within an ordination lineage). The dominance of the *Confucian* strand in the movement's public identity additionally cuts against reading the movement as Daoist-led syncretism.
- **What it does not reach:** composition does not settle classification by itself (INV-0015 §3.1 — counting strands is expressly not the decision rule); it informs CLM-0083.

# Element (c) — Rival / Counter-Reading (Steelmanned)
**The Daoist-core reading at full strength:** the synthesis is organized *around the Dao* — the movement's very name and goal-concept are Daoist vocabulary; the Mother cosmology itself has been argued (in the broader sectarian-studies literature) to carry inner-alchemical and Daoist-derived elements via Xiantiandao (cf. the Quanzhen-derived datum in SRC-0134/CLM-0084); on this reading the other traditions are absorbed *into* a fundamentally Daoist frame, making "Daoist school with syncretic reach" the better description.
**Why the claim survives:** at its strongest, this reading establishes that Daoist-derived materials are *load-bearing within the synthesis* — which the claim already asserts and CLM-0084 grades — but it does not convert the synthesis into a Daoist school's doctrine: the organizing cosmology (Eternal Mother; three-age dispensation; trans-religion pantheon) is the sect-family's own architecture, not a Daoist school's; and the movement's dominant public strand is Confucian, not Daoist (SRC-0131). The rival's true content is absorbed as CLM-0084's subject; its classificatory conclusion exceeds what composition evidence can carry (per §3.1, for either side).
**Cost to confidence:** none to the composition description itself; it enforces the claim's own refusal to grade strand proportions.
