# CLM-0085

# Yiguandao's Own Self-Understanding — Graded Strictly as a Fact About the Movement — Claims the Primordial Dao Itself, Transmitted Through a Trans-Religion Patriarchal Lineage from Remote Antiquity; in the Read Self-Presentation It Does Not Claim to Be a School of Daoism, and Its Contemporary Public Identity Leans Confucian

## Draft Claim Record

---

# Claim
> As a **fact about the movement** (INV-0015 §2.1 — this claim grades self-understanding, never the classification): Yiguandao understands itself as the bearer of the **one primordial Dao** — its English self-gloss is "The Tao that unifies all with the one" — transmitted through a **patriarchal lineage anchored in remote antiquity** (Fuxi as earliest reference; Huang Dehui, ninth patriarch, credited with establishing Xiantiandao; through to the Zhang Tianran/Sun eighteenth patriarchate), and presents itself as "the definitive and authentic Chinese form of spirituality." **In the self-presentation read live this session, it does not describe itself as a school or branch of Daoism**: its claim is to the Dao *above* the institutional religions, not to membership in one of them; and its contemporary public identity leans **Confucian** (SRC-0131). Steelmanned at full strength in its own register, this self-understanding is a coherent lineage-theology of primordiality — and it is **not the same proposition** as the Daoist-school classification the academic question concerns.

# Claim Type (KOS-0003 §3)
**Descriptive** — of the movement's self-account, in its own register; the record deliberately asserts nothing about the self-account's truth (that would breach the dual-track bracket in the confessional direction) and nothing dismissive of it (the debunking direction).

---

# Element (a) — Source & Base
- **Strongest available sourcing:** SRC-0135 — the World I-Kuan Tao Headquarters' own English self-presentation, **read live in full this session** (primary, for this register): name-gloss, Fuxi-anchored lineage, the "definitive and authentic" characterization, and the **negative datum** (no school-of-Daoism self-description on the page). SRC-0131 (description-level) corroborates the contemporary Confucian lean of the movement's public identity.
- **Language & accessibility:** the read self-presentation is English and open access; **the movement's primary internal literature is Chinese-language and was NOT surveyed — recorded as a gap (INV-0015 §2.7), not paraphrased from model knowledge.** What the wider Chinese corpus says about the movement's relation to Daoism is expressly not asserted.
- **What is and is not establishable:** the self-understanding of the World Headquarters umbrella, as published in English — establishable, live-read; the self-understanding of *all* divisions of a divisionally plural movement, or of the Chinese-language internal corpus — **not establishable from this base** (the claim's central bound).

# Element (b) — Classification Bearing
- **What this claim establishes:** the fact the revised RQ's final clause asks for — how the classification *relates to* self-understanding. The relation found is **not head-on contradiction**: the read self-account does not assert the proposition ("Yiguandao is a school of Daoism") whose academic denial would collide with it; it asserts primordial trans-religion transmission — a claim in a different register. **This observation is descriptive of the two registers' relation and carries no evidential weight for the classification in either direction (§2.1)**: that the two accounts do not collide is a fact about their *shape*, not corroboration of the scholarly placement by the self-account (which is not evidence in the scholarly register) nor of the self-account by the placement. They diverge on what the movement *is*, in registers that do not meet — and this record grades neither register by the other (Critical Review – RQ-0015 F-1 disclaimer foregrounded here).
- **What it does not reach (dual-track rule, both directions):** the self-understanding **settles nothing** about the academic classification — primordiality claims are not evidence in the scholarly register; and the classification **dismisses nothing** about the self-understanding — the lineage theology is recorded at its own full strength as the movement's operative self-account.

# Element (c) — Rival / Counter-Reading (Steelmanned)
**The strongest counter-reading here inverts the usual direction — it is the *reductionist* reading:** the self-presentation is outreach material; the "real" self-understanding is in the Chinese internal corpus and in practice, where (this reading holds) Daoist identity may be more prominent — e.g., divisions registering under Daoist umbrellas where legal environments reward it, or ritual self-location in Daoist terms; the English page is a curated face, and grading it as "the self-understanding" mistakes public relations for theology.
**Why the claim survives:** the claim's wording already carries the bound — it grades the **read** self-presentation of the **World Headquarters**, discloses the divisional plurality, and records the Chinese corpus as a gap. Within that bound the datum is solid (live-read, official, unambiguous in register). The reductionist reading, at full strength, converts into a research gap (survey the Chinese corpus and divisional practice) — it does not overturn what the read page says; and its own positive assertion (that internal self-understanding is more Daoist) has **no source in the accessible base** and so cannot be graded above it.
**Cost to confidence:** real — it is why this claim sits at Moderate with a hard scope bound rather than higher: the movement-wide generalization is expressly not made.

# Reasoning (KOS-0003 §7)
**Descriptive-inductive within a declared scope.** Live-read primary self-presentation + scholarly corroboration of the public lean → the self-understanding as stated, bounded to the read voice. **Risks checked:** *confessional drift* (nothing in this record treats the primordiality claim as evidence about the classification); *debunking drift* (nothing in this record calls the self-account error, PR, or myth — the reductionist reading is steelmanned as a scope-bound, not adopted); *over-generalization* (the movement-wide claim is refused; the gap is recorded).

# Confidence (KOS-0003 §8)
**Level 3 (Moderate)** — for the self-understanding of the read organizational voice, live-verified in full; **the movement-wide generalization is not graded** (scope bound; Chinese corpus and divisional plurality recorded as gaps per INV-0015 §2.3/§2.7).

# Limitations
- One organizational voice, English outreach register, undated page (©2003 site).
- Chinese-language internal literature unsurveyed — the deepest gap in this investigation's self-understanding track.
- Lineage-date divergences from the scholarly spine recorded in SRC-0135 §3, unresolved.

# Alternative Interpretations
1. **Reductionist/curated-face reading** — element (c): converts to a research gap; bounds but does not overturn.
2. **Daoist-identity reading** (the movement "really" understands itself as Daoist) — **no source in the accessible base asserts this**; recorded as ungraded possibility inside the gap, not as a finding.
3. **Confessional adoption** (the primordial lineage is real) — bracketed direction one; not graded.
