# 1. Research Question

**Primary (authoritative wording, owner-ratified):**

> What can be established, and at what evidential grade, about the life, deeds, and teachings of the historical Jesus of Nazareth?

Method is deliberately split out of the question (the INV-0011 pattern): *how* the answer is established — evidence layers, authenticity criteria, rival explanations — is protocol (§3), not part of the question's wording.

**Decomposition mandate (owner-ratified): six discrete, separately gradable claims** (identifiers assigned at execution, not here):

1. **Existence** of Jesus as a historical figure.
2. **Crucifixion** under Pontius Pilate.
3. **Baptism** by John the Baptist.
4. **Galilean itinerant proclamation** of the "Kingdom of God" as the central message.
5. **Authenticity and transmission of the teaching material** (parables, core sayings) — how confidently specific teachings trace to Jesus versus the early community.
6. **Reputation as exorcist and healer** — the earliest sources' report and repute; the events themselves bracketed.

**The three mandated elements — answered explicitly and separably for EACH claim** (a lesson encoded from INV-0011 criterion #2: "treated in prose" is not "answered separably"):

- **(a) ATTESTATION & LAYER:** the earliest attestation, with the specific INV-0013 evidence layer(s) it rests on and the layer-specific confidence inherited from INV-0013's graded findings.
- **(b) AUTHENTICITY CRITERIA:** which historical-critical criteria bear (multiple attestation, embarrassment, dissimilarity, coherence) and what they establish **and fail to establish** for this claim.
- **(c) RIVAL EXPLANATION:** the strongest rival — early-community invention and/or the mythicist reading as applicable — **steelmanned**, then whether the claim survives it and at what cost to confidence.

**Foundation clause (owner decision).** INV-0014 builds on INV-0013's graded evidence base (**CLM-0067…CLM-0074**) by **citation and graph edge — never re-derivation**. The pre-literary proclamation terminus is **CLM-0074, inherited** from INV-0013, not re-opened (owner decision 3; see §8 for the typed edge).

# 2. Scope & Disambiguation

## 2.1 The metaphysical bracket (KOS-0003 §3.6) — NON-NEGOTIABLE
Resurrection-as-event, miracles-as-event, and divinity are **bracketed**. Claims are phrased *"the earliest sources report/proclaim/believed X"* — never *"X happened"* **and never "X did not happen."** The slide is guarded **in both directions**: no confessional drift, no debunking assertion. The historian's tools grade attestation and repute; they do not adjudicate the metaphysical event.

## 2.2 Both poles steelmanned
The **mythicist** ("no historical Jesus") and **maximalist** ("the traditional picture largely holds") poles are each represented **at their strongest**. The historicity consensus is represented at its **actual evidential strength**, not asserted by fiat. Where evidence is split, a split verdict at honest confidence is the answer.

## 2.3 Register
Neutral, **historical-critical**. No confessional framing in any direction.

## 2.4 Boundary with INV-0001
INV-0001 (Jesus & Philosophical Daoism) compares **teachings as texts** and brackets historicity; INV-0014 addresses **exactly the historicity INV-0001 set aside**. The two are **complementary, not overlapping**.

## 2.5 Boundary with INV-0013
**Source criticism is settled in INV-0013; INV-0014 consumes it.** Any temptation to re-litigate a dating or authorship question routes to **citing INV-0013**, or — if a genuinely new evidence-need arises — is **reported as a gap**, not re-derived inline. INV-0013 and its child objects are closed records and are not modified.

## 2.6 H-band status
The H-a…H-e historical-evidence typology is available as **optional prose protocol only**, used only if genuinely useful (GB-2026-027, option b). **No frontmatter or grading-field use, no ★ anywhere, no "earned-its-place" deliverable, no STD-0008 question.**
