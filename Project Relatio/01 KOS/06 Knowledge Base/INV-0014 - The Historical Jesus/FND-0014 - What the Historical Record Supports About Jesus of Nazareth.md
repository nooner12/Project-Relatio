# 1. Statement

> **What can be established about the historical Jesus is real but layered: a minimal core at High confidence, a ministry profile at Moderate, and a rapidly falling floor beneath the specifics — with the confidence at every step set by the INV-0013 attestation layer the claim actually rests on, not by the tradition's own reach.**
>
> - **The firm core (Level 4 / High): existence and crucifixion.** A historical Jesus existed (CLM-0075) and was executed by crucifixion (CLM-0076 component i). Both rest on the contemporary Pauline layer — a writer in personal contact with the figure's brother and inner circle (Gal 1:18–19) — and on the pre-literary creed reaching back to within a few years of the event (CLM-0074, inherited). This is the strongest evidence class available for any ancient private person, and the steelmanned mythicist rival does not dislodge it.
> - **The administrative frame (Level 3 / Moderate): under Pilate.** The Pilate specification (CLM-0076 component ii) rests on the reconstructed Gospel narratives and the non-Christian notices — a Moderate layer, with field consensus running above the evidence class (a KOS-0003 §9 case, recorded, not graded in).
> - **The ministry profile (Level 3 / Moderate): baptism, proclamation, repute.** Jesus was baptized by John (CLM-0077 — the investigation's showcase divergence case: the strongest authenticity criterion in the tradition, on a modest attestation layer, with consensus far above the class); his central proclaimed theme was the Kingdom of God in a Galilean itinerant frame (CLM-0078 — theme-level multiple attestation at its broadest, plus the dissimilarity asymmetry); and he was reputed an exorcist and healer from the earliest recoverable layer, conceded even by opponents via the Beelzebul charge (CLM-0080 — with the events themselves bracketed in both directions). Each is bounded at Level 3 by the Gospel-strata layer (CLM-0069) that carries it.
> - **The teaching floor (split): characteristic voice Moderate, specific wording Low.** The tradition preserves genuinely characteristic themes, forms, and impressions of Jesus' teaching (CLM-0079 component i, Level 3) — but the authenticity of any individual saying's wording is weakly establishable (component ii, Level 2): the criteria cannot certify items (SRC-0124), memory transmits gist not verbatim (SRC-0126/SRC-0128), and community formation is demonstrable inside the tradition itself. The community-invention rival, steelmanned at full strength, **wins the item-level component and is recorded as winning it** — while failing against the theme-level asymmetries (the church wrote an idiom it did not confess; the tradition never arms Jesus for the church's own first-generation battles).
> - **What no claim reaches:** Level 5 (reserved — cross-domain historical inference does not warrant it); any narrative detail (passion chronology, miracle stories, scene details); any metaphysical event in either direction.
>
> **Both poles, at honest cost.** The **mythicist** pole is steelmanned (CLM-0075 element c, including Carrier's strongest *Ant.* 20.200 form) and fails against the Pauline acquaintance-evidence and the creed's timeline — but its pressure is why existence caps at Level 4, not 5. The **maximalist** pole is steelmanned in its two strongest contemporary forms. *Transmission form* (Gerhardsson's controlled-memorization model — engaged at CLM-0079 alt-2): fails against the observed synoptic wording-fluidity. *Source-quality form* (the eyewitness-testimony thesis — Bauckham's *Jesus and the Eyewitnesses*: the Gospels rest on named, living eyewitness custodians, so the Gospel layer deserves more than reconstructed-narrative confidence; engaged here in prose as a named rival position, per the F-1 remediation, not catalogued as an SRC): this is precisely an argument that the CLM-0069/CLM-0071 layer grades are too low — and INV-0014 consumes those grades from INV-0013 as settled (§3.1; INV-0014 §2.5). Within this investigation the thesis therefore fails structurally, not by fresh adjudication: it is an H-e argument *about* the layer, contested in the field (the formal anonymity of the Gospels — CLM-0071's High-graded finding — is the datum it must explain away), and re-litigating the layer grade would be exactly the re-derivation of INV-0013 the protocol forbids; if the field's assessment of the Gospel layer ever shifts, the revision route is a re-opened source-criticism investigation, not a silent uplift here. With both forms engaged, the record's shape *is* the refutation of both poles: too well-anchored for mythicism, too layer-bounded for maximalism.
>
> **Motivated-reasoning guard.** Three convenient stories were named and resisted: the **confessional** (letting the ministry claims borrow the core's High grade — blocked by the layer rule at every claim); the **debunking** (converting bracket-discipline into "it didn't happen" — blocked in both directions on CLM-0080 and throughout); and the **consensus-comfort** story (grading claims at the field's confidence rather than the evidence class — blocked by recording every §9 surplus separately: CLM-0076-ii, CLM-0077, CLM-0078).

# 3. Confidence (KOS-0003 §8)

**The finding is the layered map; it makes no single overall claim above its strongest component.** The core (existence; crucifixion-fact) is **High** and is High *because* it rests on the CLM-0068/CLM-0074 contemporary layer. Everything that rests on the reconstructed Gospel strata is **Moderate**, by inheritance from CLM-0069 — however strong the criteria or the consensus (the three recorded §9 surpluses mark exactly where the field's certainty outruns the evidence class). Item-level teaching authenticity is **Low**, by the demonstrated fluidity of the transmission and the instrument-level failure of the criteria. **No Level 5 anywhere** (per the investigation protocol: cross-domain historical inference does not warrant it). No averaging: split claims are reported split. The metaphysical bracket is not a confidence level — bracketed events are *ungraded in both directions*, which is a scope statement, not a low grade.

# 4. Scope & Limitations (what is NOT supported)

- **NOT supported:** any reconstruction above its layer — no Gospel narrative detail (passion chronology, trial scenes, miracle stories, birth narratives — the latter never graded at all here), no specific saying as verbatim, no itinerary map.
- **NOT supported:** the maximalist portrait ("the traditional picture largely holds") — the layer rule caps ministry claims at Moderate and items at Low.
- **NOT supported:** mythicism — steelmanned and failed against the contemporary acquaintance-layer; nor is historicity asserted by fiat (the grade rests on named evidence lines).
- **NOT supported / BRACKETED (both directions):** resurrection, miracles, and divinity **as events** — neither asserted nor denied at any level; only attestation/proclamation/repute is graded (CLM-0074 inherited; CLM-0080).
- **NOT graded:** the kingdom's precise sense (imminent vs. present); the year of execution within Pilate's prefecture; John the Baptist's own view of Jesus; non-canonical sayings sources (Thomas — a recorded gap per INV-0013 §2.2's boundary).
- **NOT a systematic review:** twelve sources (five new, seven reused from INV-0013's graded set) chosen for load-bearing coverage and verifiability; the judgments are the mainstream shape of the field with both poles steelmanned, not a meta-analytic settlement.
