# 6. Required remediation — exact fixes

**F-1 (INV-0013 §4.2, with knock-on to §4 findings-pointer, §5 verification line, and the §9 revision-history summary). The GB-2026-027 divergence verdict is overstated; the diverge/collinear coding is internally inconsistent. Downward pressure on the strength of the conclusion — reflexive (§7.6).**

The problem, precisely. The GB-2026-027 instrument (INV-0013 §3.2) asks whether the H-**band value** and the native **Level value** *diverge* (come apart) or *run collinear* (track). By that operational test:

- **Genuine band-vs-confidence divergence** (same evidence class, different confidence — band orthogonal to Level): **CLM-0070 only.** This is the real, clean, valuable result.
- **Band-vs-confidence collinear** (higher class → higher confidence): CLM-0067, CLM-0068, CLM-0073, CLM-0074 (correctly coded) — **plus CLM-0071 and CLM-0072, which are mis-coded as "diverge."** In CLM-0071 the band *tracks* the confidence (H-a → High, H-d → Low); the Specialist even writes "the confidence follows the band." In CLM-0072 the band tracks confidence across the gradient (H-a → High, H-e → Low/Moderate). **That co-variation is the collinear pattern** — indeed it is the *same* structural relationship (evidence-kind co-varying with confidence) that INV-0011 §4.2 explicitly counted as *collinear / redundant*. Coding it as "diverge" here creates a cross-investigation inconsistency that inflates the tally and drives the "materially stronger" comparison.
- **Consensus-vs-evidence divergence** (a KOS-0003 §9 phenomenon, not the band-vs-confidence question): **CLM-0069** — its genuinely surprising feature is High *consensus* exceeding H-e *evidence class*, which is §9, not GB-27's band-vs-confidence axis.

So the honest recount on the band-vs-confidence axis is roughly **1 clear divergence (CLM-0070) / 6 collinear / 1 §9 consensus-outlier (CLM-0069)** — *not* "4 diverge / 4 collinear." The equivocation is between "the two axes are conceptually distinct/diagnostically useful" (true, and CLM-0070 proves it; CLM-0071's band does real *explanatory* work) and "the two axes took divergent *values*" (true only for CLM-0070). The §3.2 definitional example the Specialist gives — "a H-d attribution with Low confidence" — is itself a **collinear** pairing (weak class → low confidence) offered as an example of divergence, which is the root of the confusion.

Apply **one** path:

- **(a) Recharacterize (reviewer-preferred under the downward-pressure mandate).** In INV-0013 §4.2, replace the "4 diverge / 4 collinear... materially STRONGER than INV-0011... four straightforwardly legible" framing with the honest recount: **one clean band-orthogonality case (CLM-0070); one §9 consensus-exceeds-evidence case (CLM-0069); and, in CLM-0071/0072, demonstrated band *diagnostic/explanatory* value under band-confidence *co-variation* (the collinear pattern).** State plainly that on the strict band-vs-confidence axis the descriptive circuit is **predominantly collinear with one clean orthogonality case**, which is **comparable in kind to INV-0011's single discrimination** (CLM-0070 is cleaner and unaided-by-review, which is a real but modest gain) — **not** a fourfold improvement. The conclusion "the typology is a coherent, diagnostically useful axis distinct from confidence" survives; the conclusion "the axes materially diverged, strengthening the standing-standard case" does not, and should be softened accordingly. Propagate to the §4 findings pointer, §5, and the §9 revision-history sentence.

# 7. Independent read on §4.2 (GB-2026-027 axis-divergence) — the reflexive deliverable

**I do NOT fully concur with the Specialist's headline.** My independent read:

1. **Did the axes genuinely diverge on descriptive claims?** *Partially — but far less than claimed.* On the strict band-vs-confidence axis the circuit is **predominantly collinear (6 of 8)** with **one clean, genuinely orthogonal case (CLM-0070: same H-e class, High vs Low)** and **one §9 consensus-vs-evidence outlier (CLM-0069)**. The two claims the Specialist counts as the "mirror" and "gradient" divergences (CLM-0071, CLM-0072) actually exhibit band-confidence *co-variation* — the collinear pattern — dressed as divergence. So the "4 diverge / 4 collinear" tally is not honest to the instrument's own operational definition.

2. **Is CLM-0070 genuinely a same-band-two-Level case, not a mislabel?** *Yes.* Markan priority and Q are both H-e (modern reconstruction) and sit at High and Low respectively. This is a real demonstration that the band does not fix the Level. It is the single most valuable datum in the circuit and is correctly coded.

3. **Is this "materially STRONGER evidence than INV-0011"?** *No, not as framed.* INV-0011 surfaced one non-redundant discrimination (CLM-0052 vs CLM-0051, review-surfaced). INV-0013 surfaces one clean orthogonality case (CLM-0070, legible without review) plus genuine *diagnostic/explanatory* value in CLM-0071. That is a modest, real improvement — cleaner and self-evident rather than review-dependent — **but it is one clean case, not four**, and the overall band-confidence balance remains predominantly collinear, as in INV-0011. The correct GB-2026-027 read is: *the H-typology is a coherent axis conceptually distinct from confidence and does real diagnostic work (locating/explaining why confidence is discounted), most cleanly at CLM-0070; it does not "materially diverge" from confidence across the board, and the honest counter-weight the Specialist already recorded (the scale adds least where evidence is strongest; prose can do much of the same work) is if anything under-stated.*

4. **Is the Specialist's honest counter-weight fair?** Mostly yes and to their credit it is present — but it does not go far enough: it concedes the four *labelled-collinear* claims are collinear, without noticing that two of the four *labelled-divergent* claims are also structurally collinear. So the counter-weight under-corrects, and the headline over-sells.

5. **Reflexive gate (§7.6).** This §4.2 assessment feeds the owner's STD-0008 decision and therefore **remains gated for reflexive use** (it may not be cited as vindication of Project Relatio's own method) pending blinded independent review. As a same-model reviewer I can flag the inflation (F-1) but cannot certify absence of a shared method-bias — and here the flag *materialised* in the flattering direction, which is exactly why the gate matters. My corrected read (predominantly collinear, one clean orthogonality case) is itself a same-model reflexive judgment and carries the same gate.

**Net for the owner:** the pair still usefully brackets the scale's domain — it earns diagnostic value on descriptive multi-class claims (CLM-0070 is the exhibit) — but the case that INV-0013 "delivered the divergence INV-0011 lacked" is overstated and should not, on its own, be read as tipping the STD-0008 decision toward a standing coded standard. The evidence supports "coherent, diagnostically useful typology" strongly and "the values genuinely diverge / a standing coded standard is necessary" weakly — which is close to where INV-0011 landed.
