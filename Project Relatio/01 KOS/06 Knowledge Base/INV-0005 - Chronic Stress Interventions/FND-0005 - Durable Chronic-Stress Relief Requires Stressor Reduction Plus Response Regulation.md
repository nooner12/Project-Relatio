# FND-0005

# Durable Chronic-Stress Relief Requires Stressor Reduction Plus Response Regulation

## Draft Finding Record

---

# 1. Statement
> **No single intervention has strong (>1-year) durability evidence for chronic-stress relief.** The most defensible strategy for *durable* relief is a **combination**: (a) **lifestyle foundations** that durably raise buffering capacity and lower cumulative physiological load — sleep (with **CBT-I** the best-evidenced durable skill), **regular exercise**, and **social connection**; (b) **response-regulation skills** (mindfulness, relaxation, slow-breathing/HRV work) for reliable *short-to-medium-term* symptom relief; and, on mechanistic grounds, (c) **acting on the stressor itself** through problem-focused coping and, where relevant, work/life change **whenever the stressor is controllable**. Techniques that only regulate the *response* reliably help in the short term but have **thin evidence of durable relief**, because they leave a persistent stressor — and its allostatic load — intact.

---

# 2. Supporting Claims
- **CLM-0013** — Mindfulness / relaxation / HRV-breathing reduce stress **short-term (High)** but **long-term durability is Low**; effects shrink under active controls (Goyal 2014). *The response-regulation leg — and the honest cap on it.*
- **CLM-0014** — Exercise reduces stress (**short-term 3–4**); its **durable stress-specific** outcome is **Low (Level 2)** (Critical Review – RQ-0005 §6.1), while its **collateral-health durability** is separately **High (Level 4)**. *A durable foundation via collateral health, not via a proven durable stress-specific effect.*
- **CLM-0015** — CBT skills, and **CBT-I especially, are durable (High for CBT-I)**; generic work-stress CBT is weaker. *The best-evidenced durable skill, via sleep.*
- **CLM-0016** — Social connection is a powerful **association (High)** with health/longevity but **interventional durability is Low**. *A foundation, under-proven as a deliberate intervention.*
- **CLM-0017** — Reducing a **controllable** stressor is **in principle central to durable relief (Moderate)** though its **interventional evidence is Low**. *The leg that makes this a combination, not a technique — and the one that cuts against a pure "regulation" framing.*
- **CLM-0018** — Nature exposure reduces **acute** stress (**Moderate**) but **durability is Speculative–Low**. *A minor short-term component only.*

---

# 3. Confidence (KOS-0003 §8)
**Level 3 (Moderate).** The synthesis is deliberately capped at its **weakest necessary link**. The finding's core structural claim — *durable relief requires combining stressor reduction with response regulation and lifestyle foundations* — depends necessarily on CLM-0017 (Moderate for the principle, Low for its interventional proof) and on the honest low-durability grades of CLM-0013/0016/0018. It therefore cannot exceed **Moderate**, and explicitly **does not** reach High.

**Two components are held more firmly, and two more weakly:**
- **Firmer (High):** that response-regulation techniques have **strong short-term but weak long-term** evidence (CLM-0013), and that **CBT-I is durably effective** for its sleep target (CLM-0015). The durability-honesty conclusion — *do not mistake an acute effect for durable relief* — is well supported.
- **Weaker (Low–Moderate):** the positive claim that **stressor reduction durably outperforms** response-regulation (CLM-0017 interventional leg) — asserted as a well-motivated principle, not a proven trial result.

---

# 4. Scope & Limitations
- **Educational evidence review, not individualized medical or psychological advice.** The individual case is bracketed.
- **Professional help is warranted** where chronic stress shades into persistent burnout, clinical depression/anxiety, or trauma; this finding does not substitute for it.
- **Controllability caveat:** the "act on the stressor" leg applies only when the stressor is **controllable**. For uncontrollable stressors (bereavement, illness, structural conditions), response-regulation and acceptance-based coping are the appropriate levers.
- **Population limitation:** much durability evidence comes from healthcare-worker occupational-stress trials; generalisation to the general adult population is a stated assumption.
- **Evidence-quality limitation:** durability evidence across the field is predominantly **low-certainty**; "up to a year" is often the ceiling of available follow-up, not a demonstrated plateau. Several effect sizes were verified only at abstract/summary level (see per-source flags). Absence of long-term evidence is treated as **uncertainty**, not disproof.
- **Motivated-reasoning disclosure (INV-0005 §6):** the interventions closest to the owner's wellness-business framing (mindfulness, breathwork) are precisely those with the **thinnest durability evidence and the largest active-control shrinkage**; the finding was not adjusted to protect that framing. Symmetrically, the anti-framing claim (CLM-0017) was **not** inflated to overcorrect — its weak interventional evidence is graded Low.
