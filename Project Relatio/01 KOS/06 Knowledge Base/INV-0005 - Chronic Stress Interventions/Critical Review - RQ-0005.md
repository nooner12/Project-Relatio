# 5. CLM-0017 ruling (primary task)

**Level 3 (Moderate) for the *principle* SURVIVES, at the floor — I do NOT require a drop to Level 2.** The claim correctly splits into the **principle (Level 3)** and the **interventional claim (Level 2)**; inflation would show in the interventional leg, which is honestly Low. The principle's backing is mixed-strength, not thin: a near-deductive core (allostatic load + persistent-input logic, ENT-0003), the transactional coping model (SRC-0022, correlational), and the honest fact that the rival — response-regulation's *own* durability — is itself thin (CLM-0013, verified).

**Two calibration Flags:** (1) **"decisively" in FND-0005 §1 overstates** a Level-3/Low leg — false precision by rhetoric (§12.1 rule 3); soften. (2) The empirical legs (SRC-0017 citation-defective, SRC-0022 unverified) are thin, so the principle rests substantially on the mechanism; keep the "well-motivated principle, not proven result" language and do not let it drift up.

**Overcorrection worry (Specialist's):** the guard worked — the interventional leg is Low, the principle's Level 3 rests on independent mechanistic grounds, not as a rhetorical counterweight. **Shared-frame caveat (§5.3):** both Specialist and I may over-value the "remove the root cause" logic because allostatic load makes it feel necessary; ENT-0003 concedes the construct is variably operationalised. A same-model reviewer cannot clear this.

**FND cap:** tracks the Level-3 principle (not the Level-2 interventional claim) — correct. Only the "decisively" wording promises more than the cap delivers.

# 6. Durability-overclaiming check

**6.1 CLM-0014 (the one grade I pushed down).** Stress-specific durable Level 3 leans on collateral-health durability + cortisol reactivity — a **category transfer** (answering durable-*stress* relief partly via durable-*health* benefit). Recommend **Level 2 for the stress-specific durable outcome**, with collateral-health durability stated as a separate, better-grounded proposition. **6.2 CLM-0013 4/2 — honest** (not artificially deflated). **6.3 CLM-0018 — appropriately conservative.** **6.4 SRC-0012** pre-post "≈0.55" vs full-text ≈0.66 — reconcile; non-load-bearing.

# 7. Two-directional motivated-reasoning finding

**(a) Under-crediting the owner's interventions to look unbiased? No.** CLM-0013 grades regulation techniques **Level 4 short-term** (generous, evidence-appropriate); the large HRV effect is acknowledged then scoped down for durability only because it has no durability follow-up. **(b) Any convenient conclusion survive unchallenged? No.** "Breathwork/mindfulness durably relieve stress" is the single most-attacked proposition; the opposite maximalist ("only structural change works") is *also* rejected. **The guard operated correctly in both directions** — subject only to the §5.3 shared-frame caveat.

Part of the §9 gate, not an internal downgrade.
