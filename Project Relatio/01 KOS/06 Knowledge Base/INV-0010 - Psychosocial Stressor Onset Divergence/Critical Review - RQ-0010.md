This pass catches overclaiming, calibration drift, uncatalogued evidence, coding steer, and citation error — **not** a blind spot we share. **INV-0010 is HEALTH / HIGH-STAKES, CHILD-FACING, and externally-consequential** (it informs Hearth & Field Pillar II age-banding). Per ROLE-0004 §5 and CON-0003 §4.5, a passed procedural review is **not** independent verification for a finding of this class; genuine independent review (a different model, or a developmental-psychology / child-program domain expert) is required before any reliance. The §7.5 gate already says this; I confirm and reinforce it.
- **Reflexive risk: LOW-to-MODERATE.** The finding does **not** flatter Project Relatio — its headline ("the field mostly measures neither construct") is deflationary toward the source literature, not self-congratulatory about the architecture. **But there is one realized motivated-reasoning vector** (§5, §7): INV-0010 *predicted* a CONFLATED majority (§3.2) and then reported the prediction "HELD strongly." That is exactly the place to check whether borderline codings were steered toward the predicted answer. They were, in part — see F-1.
- **Verification — STRONG, not total.** I independently re-verified **all 8 citations as real** (journals, DOIs/PMIDs correct) and directly confirmed the load-bearing figures against primaries or strong secondaries this session:
  - Salk, Hyde & Abramson 2017 — OR 1.95 overall, **OR 2.37 at age 12, peak OR 3.02 at 13–15**, small d at 8–11, ~1.9M people / 90+ nations (PMC5532074). **Confirmed.**
  - Gaub & Carlson 1997 — **clinic ~10:1 vs community ~3:1** (PubMed 9256583; corroborated across independent secondary sources). **Confirmed.**
  - Ramtekkar et al. 2010 — **overall M:F 2.28:1**, females under-diagnosed through clinical channels (PMC3101894). **Confirmed.**
  - Moffitt & Caspi 2001 — **10:1 childhood-onset vs 1.5:1 adolescence-onset** (PubMed 11393651). **Confirmed exactly.**
  - Angold, Costello & Worthman 1998 — girls exceed boys at **Tanner Stage III+**; **pubertal status a better predictor than chronological age** (Psychological Medicine 28(1):51–61). **Confirmed** — and the peer-stress *mediation* literature the Specialist cites to cap the claim is real (PMC2704098; PMC3349967).
  - Lewinsohn et al. 1998 — **"at age 6, females already ~2× as likely to have had an anxiety disorder"** (PubMed 9505043). **Confirmed verbatim.**
  - **One figure taken partly on trust:** Allen et al. 2013 (SRC-0064) — citation, journal, DOI and general direction (female symptoms rise across adolescence, male stable/declining) confirmed, but I did **not** open the primary to the specific "rise 14→peak 17" trajectory numbers this session. # 2. Steelman (given before attacking)
The investigation is disciplined and, on its central move, correct. It does the hard thing: it treats "sex difference" not as a fact but as a **coding decision to be audited**, and it builds an operative rule (a CONFLATED/UNRESOLVED source cannot carry a sex-attribution at any level) that it then actually enforces — six of seven claims are held to gender-patterned/unattributed language even where a biological reading would be tidier. The strongest element (depression timing, CLM-0044, Level 4) is genuinely one of the most replicated findings in developmental psychopathology, and the Specialist *withholds* causal attribution from it despite its strength. The one biology-linked claim (CLM-0045) is capped at Level 3 with the mechanism explicitly left open and the peer-stress mediation literature cited *against* the convenient biological reading. The map form (five loci, each with its own timing signature and confidence level) correctly refuses to collapse into a ranking, and the finding makes no claim above its strongest component. The gate is declared at opening, carried verbatim at closing, and the INV-0008 non-inference is airtight. This is careful work; the flags below are sharpening and honesty-calibration, not demolition.

# 3. The adversarial pass (the brief's named tests)

**Test A — the SRC-0060 / CLM-0045 pivot (the crux). Verdict: SEX-MEASURED stands; census stays 7/1; framing must be sharpened. Does NOT collapse to 8/8.**
The counter to press was: pubertal status is a *maturational* variable, not "biological sex," and the divergence may still be gender-mediated — so recode to CONFLATED/UNRESOLVED and zero sources carry sex-attribution. I pressed it and it does **not** succeed as a recode, for one decisive reason: Angold physically **measured a biological maturational variable** (Tanner staging) and showed the divergence *timing* tracks it over age — something **none** of the other seven sources did (they measured only a label). That methodological fact is real and worth preserving in the census; erasing it into UNRESOLVED would be *less* accurate, not more. **SEX-MEASURED is defensible as "a biological variable was measured."**
**However**, the counter exposes a genuine framing defect that must be fixed (F-2): puberty occurs in **both** sexes, so measuring pubertal stage does **not** establish that the *between-sex* divergence is biologically caused — the operative unknown is the **sex × puberty interaction** (why mid-puberty raises girls' but not boys' depression), which is unmeasured. The code's own definition ("divergence attributed to it *on a stated mechanism*") is therefore only half-satisfied: the maturational marker is measured, the mechanism of the sex difference is not. CLM-0045 mostly says this already (mechanism unresolved, social mediation flagged, capped at Level 3), so the **confidence level is fine** — but two phrasings over-read it and must be corrected (see F-2). The pivot resolves in the Specialist's favor on the *code*, against the Specialist on two *framings*.

**Test B — the two weakest claims (CLM-0046 anxiety; CLM-0050 modal expression).**
- **CLM-0046 (anxiety, Level 2 → 3).** The Level-2 cap on the "≈age 6" figure is honest (retrospective recall, single cohort — Reliability 2, Independence 2), and I verified the age-6 figure verbatim. **One flag (F-3):** the Level-3 *direction* reach ("anxiety diverges earlier than depression") is partly warranted by *uncatalogued* "convergent background reviews located this session, not separately catalogued." That is an evidentiary basis the record cannot inspect. The fix is easy because it is unnecessary: the direction is already supported by the **contrast between two catalogued sources** — SRC-0061 (anxiety skew by ~age 6) vs SRC-0059/CLM-0044 (depression skew at 12–13). Rest the Level-3 reach on that catalogued contrast and drop the appeal to uncited reviews.
- **CLM-0050 (modal expression, Level 2 → 3).** Honestly scoped as a single-domain *tendency*, modest magnitude, generalized cautiously. It also leans on "the broader literature" (uncatalogued) — but there it is used to **lower** the claim (modest effect sizes), which is conservative and creates no upward-pressure problem. **Conformant, note only.**

**Test C — the closest CONFLATED calls (SRC-0064 eating; SRC-0066 form) and the UNRESOLVED question. Primary finding, F-1.**
This is my most important finding. **The census reports ZERO of eight sources as UNRESOLVED.** For a corpus in which the Specialist's own limitation notes repeatedly say the mechanism "could be sociocultural, pubertal biology, or reporting" (SRC-0064) or "neurodevelopmental, socialization-driven, or ascertainment-influenced" (SRC-0065), a literal zero in UNRESOLVED is not credible — it is the signature of a coding boundary applied in the prediction's favor. The scheme's definitions matter: **CONFLATED = "sex labels applied to *gender* data"** (presupposes the underlying data is gendered), whereas **UNRESOLVED = "cannot be determined from the paper as written."**
- Where CONFLATED is *strong and correct*: **SRC-0062, SRC-0063 (ADHD).** These papers **positively demonstrate** the sex label captures a gendered/social ascertainment process. Textbook CONFLATED. Verified. ✓
- Where CONFLATED is **over-coded**: **SRC-0064 (eating), SRC-0065 (conduct).** By the Specialist's own notes, biology and socialization are *both* live and *undetermined*. That is the definition of **UNRESOLVED**, not "sex-label-on-gender-data." "Not SEX-MEASURED" was silently treated as "therefore CONFLATED," which skips UNRESOLVED entirely.
- Borderline-but-tolerable: **SRC-0059** (bare epidemiological tag) and **SRC-0066** (form divergence, where socialization is at least the *dominant* theory). I do not require recoding these, but they carry the same tension.
**Consequence:** a well-calibrated coding of this corpus would place **at least SRC-0064 and SRC-0065 in UNRESOLVED**, making the census roughly **CONFLATED 5 / UNRESOLVED 2 / SEX-MEASURED 1**, and the §3.2 prediction "CONFLATED-majority, HELD strongly" should be **softened**. Crucially — and this is why F-1 is a Flag, not a Block — **no downstream claim changes**, because the operative rule treats CONFLATED and UNRESOLVED identically (neither can carry a sex-attribution). The finding's substantive answer is not weakened; it is **strengthened and made more honest**: the robust, defensible result is the *corollary* the Specialist already stated — **"neither construct is typically measured"** — and that should be promoted from corollary to headline, replacing the cleaner-than-warranted "7 CONFLATED, prediction HELD strongly."

**Test D — independent figure re-verification of the two sources not opened at full text.** Done (see §1). **Gaub & Carlson 10:1/3:1 and Moffitt & Caspi 10:1/1.5:1 both independently confirmed.** No discrepancy.

**Test E — vocabulary and scope.**
- "**critical period**" appears **only** in the protocol's own exclusion rule and acceptance criterion — **never** as an unearned descriptor in a claim, finding, or source. ✓
- **No superlative / magnitude ranking is asserted**; "biggest/greatest/most significant" appear only inside disclaimers and risk-checks. "Severely impaired" in CLM-0047 is a *source-quoted clinical descriptor* of referred girls, not a stressor-magnitude ranking. ✓
- FND-0010 §3 "strongest single element" / "best-supported" are explicitly tied to **Level 4** (native confidence), i.e., ranking *claims by epistemic warrant* — permitted — **not** ranking *stressors by magnitude/prevalence*. **No smuggled magnitude ranking.** ✓
- "Earliest signature" (anxiety) orders loci by **onset timing**, which is the mapped variable itself — not a magnitude claim. ✓
- **Scope 5–17 held:** where a source ran wider (Allen 14–20; Ramtekkar 7–29; Ettekal & Ladd grades 1–11), the *claims* are bounded to the in-band portion (e.g., CLM-0048 uses 14–17, not 14–20). ✓

# 4. Independence / proponent-program risk
No single research program dominates the corpus — eight sources span depression epidemiology, developmental epidemiology, ADHD meta-analysis + community replication, an eating-disorder cohort, the Dunedin antisocial program, and a peer-relations cohort. The ADHD claim (CLM-0047) is the best-defended precisely because it rests on **two independent designs** (a pooled meta-analysis and a separate community sample) converging. The single-cohort claims (CLM-0045 Angold; CLM-0048 Allen; CLM-0049 Moffitt/Dunedin) are **disclosed as single-cohort and capped accordingly** — none exceeds Level 3, and each names cohort-specificity. No over-claim on independence. The real independence limit is **not** in the sources but in the **review**: procedural-only, same-model (see §1, §10).

# 5. Motivated reasoning
The convenient conclusions were mostly named and resisted — the "it's just puberty/biology" reading is bounded at CLM-0045 with peer-stress mediation flagged; the "it's all referral bias, no real difference" over-correction is refused at CLM-0047 (residual gap left open); the perennialist "sex and gender are the same" collapse is what the whole scheme exists to prevent. **The one prior that *did* leak is the investigation's own prediction.** A construct-provenance study that predicts "the field mostly conflates" has a standing incentive to *find* conflation, and the zero-UNRESOLVED census (F-1) is where that incentive shows: undetermined-mechanism cases were resolved toward the predicted CONFLATED rather than the more honest UNRESOLVED. Naming this is the core service of this review. The remedy (F-1) does not overturn the finding — it makes the finding's honesty match its rigor.

# 7. Required remediation — exact fixes

**F-1 (FND-0010 §1(c) + §2 census + INV-0010 §4/§5 census + SRC-0064, SRC-0065). Coding steer toward the predicted CONFLATED; zero UNRESOLVED is not credible.**
Apply **either** path:
- **(a) Recode (preferred):** change SRC-0064 and SRC-0065 construct-provenance codes to **UNRESOLVED**; update the census everywhere it appears to **CONFLATED 5 / UNRESOLVED 2 / SEX-MEASURED 1 / GENDER-MEASURED 0**; and rewrite the §3.2 prediction verdict from "CONFLATED-majority HELD strongly" to something like: *"The prediction that the majority of sources would fail to cleanly measure a construct HELD (7 of 8); the finer split shows most are not demonstrably 'sex-label-on-gender-data' (CONFLATED) but construct-undetermined (UNRESOLVED). The robust result is the corollary: neither construct is typically measured."* **No claim text changes** — the operative rule is identical for CONFLATED and UNRESOLVED.
- **(b) Defend:** retain CONFLATED for SRC-0064/0065 only by adding, per source, positive evidence that the underlying data is specifically *gender* data (not merely "no biological mechanism measured"). This is strong for ADHD (already made) and hard to make for eating/conduct; if it cannot be made, use path (a).
Either way, **soften "HELD strongly"** in FND-0010 §1(c) and the INV-0010 §4/§5 census and revision notes.

**F-2 (CLM-0045 + FND-0010 §1(b) and §4). Do not let SEX-MEASURED / "biological-timing" read as established biological-sex causation.**
- FND-0010 §4 first bullet currently reads: *"NOT supported: that any of these divergences (except the bounded puberty–depression timing) is caused by biological sex."* The parenthetical implies the puberty–depression case **is** a biological-sex causation. Replace with: *"NOT supported: that any of these divergences is caused by biological sex — including depression's, where the timing tracks a measured maturational variable (pubertal status) but the sex × puberty mechanism is unresolved and substantially socially mediated."*
- CLM-0045 / SRC-0060: add one sentence making explicit that **puberty occurs in both sexes**, so measuring pubertal stage does **not** establish that the between-sex divergence is biologically caused; what is licensed is only "timing co-varies with a measured maturational variable." (Most of this is present; make the both-sexes point explicit so the census's "1 SEX-MEASURED" cannot be read as "1 confirmed biological sex cause.")
- **Confidence level 3 is correct and unchanged.**

**F-3 (CLM-0046). Remove reliance on uncatalogued evidence.**
Rewrite the Level-3 *direction* warrant to rest on the catalogued contrast (SRC-0061 anxiety skew ~age 6 vs SRC-0059/CLM-0044 depression skew 12–13); delete the sentence appealing to "developmental-epidemiology reviews located this session, not separately catalogued" as a load-bearing basis. **Confidence unchanged** (Level 2 → 3 direction stands on the catalogued contrast).

No confidence level is raised anywhere (a reviewer cannot; OPS-0003 §4). No level required to drop; the flags are coding-honesty and framing, and F-1's recode leaves every claim's confidence intact.
