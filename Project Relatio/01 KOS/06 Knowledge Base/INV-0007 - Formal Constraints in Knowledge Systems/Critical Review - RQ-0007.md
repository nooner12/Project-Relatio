# 1. Honest limits (read first)

**1.1 Procedural independence only (ROLE-0004 §5).** The reviewer and Specialist are the **same underlying model**, separately invoked. This catches overclaiming, calibration drift, uncatalogued evidence, arithmetic, and citation error — and did (§§3–7). It **cannot** clear a blind spot the two of us share. That limit is unusually load-bearing *for this specific investigation*, because the conclusion is **self-flattering to Project Relatio itself** (a governed, formally constrained knowledge system), and I am part of Project Relatio's own tooling. A disposition to find "well-designed constraints are good, and our merit principle is vindicated" congenial is precisely the kind of shared prior a same-model reviewer may not escape. This is named in §6 and only genuine independent review can clear it.

**1.2 Verification strength — MIXED; partially verification-light (§7.5).**
- **Live re-verified this session:** Simmons et al. 2011 — citation (Psychol Science 22(11):1359–1366) confirmed, the **combined 61%** false-positive figure confirmed ("inflate…to over 60%"), per-degree rates confirmed in the **~7.7–12.6%** band (optional-stopping ≈ +50% over 5% → ~7.7%; gender covariate **11.7%** confirmed). Adler & Borys 1996 — citation (ASQ 41(1):61–89) confirmed; the **four design features** (repair, internal transparency, global transparency, flexibility) confirmed. Ostrom 1990 — the **design-principle set** (congruence, collective-choice arrangements, monitoring, graduated sanctions, nested enterprises) confirmed as used.
- **NOT re-verified this session:** March 1991 simulation specifics; Campbell's verbatim wording; Meyer & Rowan; Hansen pagination; the Szollosi verbatim quote; and the DOIs/ISBNs the Specialist already flagged as not independently re-verified. I independently confirmed that the **Adler & Borys full-text PDF is not machine-readable** (binary), corroborating the Specialist's flag on SRC-0035.
- **Fabrication check:** **no fabricated source or figure found in the sampled subset.** All eight sources are real, correctly attributed, and cited at standard pagination. This is **not** a §12.1 Block situation.

**1.3 Stakes.** This is a **general literature-synthesis** question — not health/clinical, not individualized advice, not otherwise externally consequential in the ordinary §7.5 sense. The reliance concern here is **not** third-party safety; it is **reflexive** (the finding indirectly endorses the vault's own governance). That shifts where the gate bites (§8).

# 2. What I attacked

Reasoning risks (KOS-0003 §7) across seven claims, one entity, one finding, eight sources; the 0–5 grades (§5), with focus on the **independence** grades where "convergence of independent traditions" is doing warrant-work; the confidence cap and whether the synthesis exceeds its weakest necessary leg (§8); the analogical-transfer assumption (the Specialist's own nominated weakest point); the steelmanning of alternatives; and — the primary brief — whether the **self-flattering** direction survived without a convenient prior, checked by tracing the synthesis's *actual wording* back to its cited sources.

# 3. Per-dimension analysis

## 3.1 Reasoning risks (KOS-0003 §7)

Generally well-guarded. False-dichotomy risk is handled repeatedly (enabling/coercive named as a continuum; codification/personalization named as a mix). Confirmation bias toward "constraints are good" is genuinely resisted: the four failure-mode claims (CLM-0030/0031/0032 and the tacit-cost half of 0029) are retained at full weight, and CLM-0030 explicitly keeps the *inconvenient* half. Analogical overreach is flagged on every affected claim rather than buried.

Two residual reasoning concerns:

- **Abstraction-level convergence presented as evidential independence (analogical-reasoning risk, KOS-0003 §7).** CLM-0032 and CLM-0033 both grade **Independence 4** on the argument that "genuinely different traditions converge." But the convergence lives at the level of the *researcher's chosen abstraction* ("enabling / fitted / revisable / constraints crowd out real work"), not at the level of the phenomena. March models socialization to a shared code; Szollosi argues about theory-mapping of preregistration; Ostrom measures institutional longevity of resource commons; Adler measures worker attitudes to procedures. At a high enough abstraction, many governance traditions will "rhyme" with "adaptive, participatory governance beats rigid imposition" — that near-truism is the motte. Grading that rhyme as Independence 4 rewards the abstraction the synthesizer selected. Because confidence is already capped at Moderate this does not inflate the confidence *number*, but the **Independence 4 grades on CLM-0032 and CLM-0033 are generous** and the "three independent traditions converge" framing overstates the evidential independence. (In CLM-0032 the two anchors in fact support *two different* sub-claims — ossification vs. method-as-false-proxy — so they are complementary, not corroborating the same proposition.)

- **Prestige-anchored reliability grades (argument-from-authority-adjacent).** Reliability 4s lean on "seminal," "most-cited," "Nobel-recognised." Citation count/prestige is not reliability *for the analogical use* actually being made. The records partly correct this via lower Quality grades and explicit transfer-flags, so it is bounded — but the reliability column is measuring source prestige, not fitness-for-this-inference.

## 3.2 Evidence grading (KOS-0003 §5)

Mostly defensible and, where weak, honestly low (CLM-0028 Independence 2, Quality 3 is exemplary candour). Two systemic observations:

- **Single-catalogued-anchor pattern.** CLM-0028 (Adler only), CLM-0029 (Hansen only), CLM-0030 (Meyer & Rowan only), CLM-0031 (Campbell only) each rest on one catalogued source, with corroboration described as "widely reported; not separately catalogued." Each instance is disclosed, and Moderate grades are appropriate — but the **cumulative** effect is that a large share of the investigation's robustness rests on uncatalogued secondary corroboration a reviewer cannot check. This correctly caps confidence at Moderate; I note it as a property of the evidence base, not a defect to escalate.
- **Independence 4 grades** (CLM-0032, CLM-0033) are generous per §3.1.

## 3.3 Confidence calibration (KOS-0003 §8)

**Well-calibrated and appropriately deflationary.** No claim reaches Level 5; the reserve is correctly justified for cross-domain interpretive synthesis. The FND-0007 **Level 3 cap** is correctly constructed: the synthesis necessarily depends on the six Moderate claims, and the single Level-4 leg (CLM-0027) rightly does not lift it. CLM-0027 at **Level 4** is defensible — the 61% is verified and the claim is bounded to error control. This is the calibration done right; I apply no downward pressure to the confidence *levels* themselves. My flags below concern **wording that outruns the (correct) confidence**, and **independence grades**, not the levels.

## 3.4 Assumptions & bracketing (KOS-0003 §10)

Strong. The analogical-transfer assumption is named as "the single most contestable move" and repeated on every affected claim; the selection assumption (8 anchors for 3 vast literatures) names specific omitted sources (Bozeman, Goodhart/Strathern, path-dependence). This is model behaviour. **One asymmetry the bracketing misses:** the named omissions (Goodhart, red-tape, lock-in) would mostly *reinforce* the failure-mode side. The side that lacks a catalogued skeptic is the **synthesis (CLM-0033)** — see §3.5.

## 3.5 Steelmanned alternatives

Alternatives are engaged, not strawmanned (the red-tape/quantity counter, the "codification enables exploration" counter, the "decoupling is adaptive" nuance are all conceded as partial). **But the synthesis has no catalogued adversarial anchor.** CLM-0033's alternative #1 — that rigid, non-revisable constraints are sometimes exactly right (safety-critical standards, fraud controls) — is *conceded from reason* but anchored to **no source**, while every *failure-mode* claim gets a catalogued anchor. The strongest opponent of "enabling/fitted/revisable is the resolution" is the observation that much of the most valuable formal machinery in knowledge systems is deliberately **rigid and non-participatory** (measurement standards, cryptographic protocols, controlled vocabularies, safety-critical checklists) — and that literature is neither catalogued nor weighed. The synthesis is steelmanned against quantity-arguments but not against **rigidity-sometimes-wins**.

## 3.6 Motivated reasoning (the primary brief)

The Specialist claims to have "confined reflexive relevance to one bracketed implication in FND-0007 §5." **That claim does not fully hold, and this is my central finding.** The convenient prior did not just touch the bracketed implication — **it leaked into the vocabulary of the synthesis claim itself.**

- CLM-0033 body: constraints should "**earn its place on demonstrated use rather than on symmetry, completeness, or its own appearance of rigour**." FND-0007 §4 repeats it. This is a **near-verbatim import of Project Relatio's own merit principle** (CLAUDE.md / ADR-GOV-0002: "earns its place on its merits… justified by use, not by symmetry or completeness"). **None of the three cited sources — Adler & Borys, Ostrom, Szollosi — uses "earn its place on demonstrated use" or "symmetry or completeness."** That framing is the *vault's* frame, not the literature's, presented as an output of the literature.
- FND-0007 §5 then states the synthesis "**independently supports** a merit/use principle of the kind such systems sometimes adopt." Articulating the literature in the vault's own vocabulary and then declaring that it "independently supports" the vault's principle is **not independent support** — it is the convenient conclusion restated. The guard (naming the flattery, retaining the failure modes) is *partly* real, but the vocabulary leak means the synthesis is **not framed neutrally**, and the §5 implication edges from "offered, not an audit" toward self-vindication.

This is a **Flag, not a Block**: the underlying literature genuinely does support enabling/fitted/revisable design, and the failure-mode claims are retained at equal weight (the guard is not theatre). The defect is that the *articulation* imports the owner's frame and the §5 implication overclaims independence. It is exactly the kind of thing a same-model reviewer is worst-placed to catch and must therefore flag explicitly for genuine independent review (§8).

# 6. Shared-frame caveat (same-model limit)

Beyond the individual flags, the Specialist and I may share two dispositions this investigation cannot self-clear: (i) treating the enabling/coercive and exploration/exploitation lenses as *the* correct frame rather than *a* frame; and, more sharply, (ii) finding the **self-flattering conclusion** congenial because we are both instruments of the very system the conclusion flatters. §3.6 shows the flattery already reached the synthesis wording. A same-model review cannot certify that it caught all of it. This is the one finding I most want a **different model or human** to re-run.

---

# 7. Overall verdict

**CONFORMANT WITH FLAGS. No Blocked objects.** The investigation is epistemically sound in its bones: the KOS-0003 pipeline is followed, confidence is honestly capped at Moderate and never reaches Level 5, the analogical-transfer limitation is named relentlessly, the failure-mode claims are retained at full weight, and no fabrication exists in the sampled subset (the one hard number, 61%, is verified). The flags are about **wording that outruns correct confidence**, **two generous independence grades**, **uncatalogued companions**, **an over-claimed entity centrality**, and — most importantly — a **motivated-reasoning vocabulary leak** that the Specialist believed was fully bracketed and was not.

# 8. Required remediations (priority order)

1. **FND-0007 §4/§5 + CLM-0033 (motivated reasoning).** Remove or attribute the vault-frame phrasing "**earn its place on demonstrated use… symmetry, completeness, or its own appearance of rigour**" — it is Project Relatio's own merit principle, not a finding of Adler/Ostrom/Szollosi. Soften FND §5's "**independently supports** a merit/use principle" to acknowledge that articulating the literature in the vault's vocabulary is **not** independent support. (KOS-0003 §7 confirmation bias / §10.)
2. **FND-0007 title.** "…**Not on Their Amount**" → "not *primarily* on their amount," to match CLM-0028's body and its conceded quantity counter. (§12.1 rule 3, false precision by rhetoric.)
3. **CLM-0033 (steelman + independence).** Either catalogue a source for the conceded "rigid/non-revisable constraints are sometimes right" counter (safety-critical/standards literature), or explicitly record that the synthesis's chief opponent is uncatalogued; and re-examine the **Independence 4** grade given abstraction-level convergence (§3.1, §3.5).
4. **CLM-0032.** Re-grade or annotate **Independence 4**: March and Szollosi anchor *different* sub-claims and one is a contested minority view — "converge" overstates.
5. **ENT-0005.** Soften "the conceptual hinge of the whole synthesis" to "one of the load-bearing design constructs (the enabling leg)"; the fitted/revisable legs and the failure modes are anchored elsewhere.
6. **CLM-0031 / CLM-0029.** Catalogue the invoked companions (Merton for goal-displacement; Polanyi/Nonaka for tacit-loss/context-stripping) or explicitly demote those sub-claims to uncatalogued invocations — the claim titles currently assert what the uncatalogued companions carry.
7. **CLM-0027 / SRC-0037 (minor).** Report the per-degree band as ~8–13% (optional stopping ≈7.7%), or retain "approximate" — currently "~9–13%" is slightly high at the low end.
