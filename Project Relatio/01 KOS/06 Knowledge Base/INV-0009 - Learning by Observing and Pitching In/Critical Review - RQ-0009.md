# 2. Steelman (given before attacking)
LOPI is **not folk advocacy.** It rests on 60+ years of comparative ethnography **plus** two controlled attention experiments with **out-group comparisons** (European American / Western-schooled) and a **cross-population replication** (Guatemalan Mayan → U.S. Mexican-heritage). It **names the community structures** that make it work (the seven-facet, community-organized prism) rather than claiming a universal method, and it **explicitly corrects** the "nonverbal / no-teaching" caricature (Paradise & Rogoff 2009). For its own communities, the base finding is genuinely well-supported, and the shared-attention mechanism has real experimental teeth. The investigation gives this steelman fairly (CLM-0041/0042 at Level 3, reaching 4 for the experimental component) — it does not strawman LOPI to make the transfer limit look bigger.

---

# 3. The four counter-cases (the brief's adversarial pass)

**Counter-case 1 — selection/confound.** The experiments compare groups by **cultural background / maternal Western-schooling**, not random assignment; the attention effect is entangled with language, kinship, subsistence, and multi-age structure. **Handled well:** CLM-0041 Alt-1 and CLM-0042 Alt-1 both concede this as the reason the grade is Moderate not High, and the assumptions section names it. *Residual note (not a flag):* Level 3 leans on **convergence** (ethnography + two-population replication + out-group contrast), not on any single clean design — which is the honest basis for Moderate. I apply no downward pressure.

**Counter-case 2 — transfer failure.** A single-child suburban home lacks the multi-age cohort and genuinely shared productive work. **This is the investigation's strongest section (CLM-0043):** it correctly identifies that community-organization, mixed-age, and *learning-from-interactions-directed-to-others* facets have no substrate, and that the **best-evidenced** mechanism is precisely the one that degrades — a genuinely sharp, non-obvious result. **One flag (F-1, sharpening):** the Level-2 *reach* is attached to "observation of, **and contribution to**, a parent's real work." Observation transfers reasonably; but **genuinely shared, consequential productive work is scarce in a modern single-child home** (most household tasks are brief and individual, unlike subsistence endeavors), so the **contribution-to-a-shared-endeavor** facet has thinner substrate than the observation facet. The Level-2 reach should attach **mainly to observation**, with the contribution facet flagged as weaker still. See §7.

**Counter-case 3 — romanticization.** Does the finding drift into "instruction is unnecessary"? **No — actively guarded.** SRC-0056's judicious-speech correction is carried; CLM-0041, CLM-0043, and FND §1(d)/§4 all explicitly refuse the anti-instruction and the "include your kid in real life = LOPI" readings. This is the popular misreading (LOPI is frequently cited by unschooling advocates) and the investigation refuses it cleanly. **Conformant.**

**Counter-case 4 — measurement.** What competence is actually measured? Attention to, and one-week reproduction of, a **specific toy-construction demonstration** — narrow. **Handled well:** CLM-0042 and SRC-0057/0058 name the narrow outcome; FND §1(b) foregrounds it; CLM-0041 honestly splits the *broad* "real, valued competence" (ethnographic) from the *narrow* experimentally-measured component (reaching Level 4 only for the latter). That split — rigor from the experiments, breadth from the ethnography, not conflated — is exactly right. **Conformant.**

---

# 4. Independence / proponent-program risk
Four of five sources are Rogoff-program; the framework (SRC-0054) is proponent-authored. **Disclosed** (CLM-0041/0042 Independence 3; INV §9). The out-group experimental comparisons are the main independent check, and they are real — but broader **independent replication by non-Rogoff labs** would be needed to lift the base finding above Moderate. The investigation does not over-claim here; Level 3 is the right ceiling given the single-program concentration. **Note, not a flag** — the grade already reflects it.

---

# 5. Motivated reasoning
The consequential prior in *this* topic is homeschool-sympathetic: "children learn by participating, so a home-ed child will get LOPI benefits." The investigation reads **against** it — the transfer claim is held to **Level 1 (→2)**, explicitly **not** inflated by the Moderate base grades, and the finding's headline is that the **best-evidenced mechanism travels worst.** That is the convenient conclusion being actively denied, not smuggled. The guard holds.
