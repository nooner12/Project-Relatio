# Baluška & Reber 2019 — "Sentience and Consciousness in Single Cells" (the Cellular Basis of Consciousness thesis)

## Draft Source Record

---

# 1. Identification
František Baluška & Arthur S. Reber, "Sentience and consciousness in single cells: how the first minds emerged in unicellular species," *BioEssays* 41(3):1800229 (2019) — the **Cellular Basis of Consciousness (CBC)** thesis: all cellular life is sentient from life's origin, grounded in excitable membranes, oscillating cytoskeletal polymers, and flexible proteins (further developed in the 2023/24 book *The Sentient Cell*, OUP, with W. Miller). **In this KB it is the strong-enthusiast / near-panpsychist pole, used strictly to steelman the maximal reading of CLM-0066 — and it is the position the investigation brackets, not adopts** (rung (e) sentience).

# 2. Source Evaluation (KOS-0003 §6)
- **Authority:** Mixed-high. Baluška (plant cell biology) and Reber (implicit-cognition psychology) are serious scientists; *BioEssays* is peer-reviewed. But CBC is an **avowedly speculative, theory-first thesis**, not an empirical result.
- **Transparency:** High about being a bold hypothesis.
- **Independence:** Strongly committed to the maximal "sentience is coterminous with life" prior — a motivated-reasoning vector that must be named and quarantined; it cannot be used to *establish* fungal sentience, only to represent the pole.
- **Intent:** To argue sentience is original to life, not a late neural emergent.

# 3. Limitations (what this source does NOT establish)
- Asserts, does **not demonstrate**, cellular sentience; provides **no fungal-specific empirical test**. It is a philosophical/theoretical position, and directly contested by deflationary critics (SRC-0081 authors, Taiz).
- Because it makes sentience true by definition of "life," it **cannot discriminate** degrees of cognition — which is exactly why CLM-0066 uses Lyon's graded framework (SRC-0087) for grading and holds CBC at the bracketed rung (e).

# 4. Key Content / Passages Used
- CBC: **all cells are sentient/agential** from origin; the membrane creates a subjective interior vs. objective exterior; excitability, cytoskeletal oscillation, and protein flexibility are the proposed substrates.
- Represents the **strong end** of the enthusiast pole against which the deflationary pole (mere chemistry) is balanced.
