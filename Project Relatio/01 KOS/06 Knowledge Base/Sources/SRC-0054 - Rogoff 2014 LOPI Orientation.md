# SRC-0054

# Rogoff 2014 — Learning by Observing and Pitching In: An Orientation

## Draft Source Record

---

# 1. Identification
Rogoff, B. (2014), "Learning by Observing and Pitching In to family and community endeavors: An orientation," *Human Development* 57(2-3):69–81 (Karger). The **defining framework statement** for LOPI: a holistic model of **seven interrelated facets** (diagrammed as a prism to stress interrelation) — (1) community organization of learning; (2) motivation (children's own initiative to contribute); (3) social organization of endeavors (collaborative, flexible, mixed ages); (4) goals (transformation of participation / belonging); (5) learning through **wide, keen attention**, sometimes with guidance; (6) communication coordinated through **shared reference** in shared endeavors (verbal *and* nonverbal); (7) assessment (through contribution/feedback in the activity). Citation and the seven-facet structure verified this session via the Karger record and multiple secondary summaries.

# 2. Source Evaluation (KOS-0003 §6)
- **Authority:** High — the field-defining orientation article by the framework's originator, in a peer-reviewed developmental journal; introduces the special-issue collection on LOPI.
- **Transparency:** High — the facets and their interrelation are explicitly laid out; the model is presented as descriptive of documented communities, not as a universal prescription.
- **Independence:** Moderate — single-author orientation by the framework's principal proponent (a proponent-authored definition, by design).
- **Intent:** Define and organize LOPI as a coherent construct.

# 3. Limitations (what this source does NOT establish)
- It is a **framework/orientation**, not primary empirical evidence: it organizes and interprets a body of ethnographic and experimental work rather than reporting a new study or effect size.
- The seven facets are explicitly a **constellation** — the model itself stresses they are interrelated and community-embedded, which means the framework **cannot** license decomposing LOPI into a single portable ingredient (relevant to the transfer sub-claim, CLM-0043).
- Proponent-authored: the strongest independent test of LOPI's claims lives in the experimental sources (SRC-0057, SRC-0058), not here.
- **Funding:** not stated/verified this session.

# 4. Key Content / Passages Used
- LOPI = children's initiative in contributing to ongoing family/community activity, combined with others' collaborative guidance of that initiative.
- Facet 1 (**community organization of learning**) and facet 3 (**mixed-age, collaborative endeavors**) are the community structures the transfer question (CLM-0043) targets.
- Facet 5: learning through **keen observation, sometimes with guidance** — explicitly *not* "instruction is unnecessary."
