# CLM-0066

# On the Cognition Ladder, Fungal Evidence Lands Firmly at Rungs (a) Stimulus–Response and (b) Adaptive Foraging / Information Integration (High), Reaches Only Operationally and Contestably Into (c) Learning/Memory (Low), and Warrants (d) "Basal/Minimal Cognition" ONLY as a Contested Interpretive Framing Rather Than a Settled Fact (Low) — With (e) Sentience/Consciousness Explicitly BRACKETED as Undetermined; Plus a Bounded Note on What Would Even COUNT as "Thought" Here

## Draft Claim Record

> **This is the investigation's verdict claim. It integrates the descriptive base (CLM-0059/0060/0062), the memory claim (CLM-0064), and the analogy (CLM-0065), and grades them against the §1 ladder. It may not exceed its weakest necessary link. Rung (e) is bracketed, not graded.**

---

# Claim
> Graded against the INV-0012 §1 ladder, the current fungal evidence supports:
> - **(a) Stimulus–response — High.** Uncontested (hyphae respond to chemical/physical/electrical stimuli).
> - **(b) Adaptive foraging / information integration — High.** Well-evidenced (CLM-0059, CLM-0060, CLM-0062): resource-directed remodelling and whole-colony integration.
> - **(c) Learning & memory — Low.** Only an operational carry-over (CLM-0064); cognitive learning in fungi is largely unestablished.
> - **(d) Minimal / basal cognition — Low, as a contested interpretive *framing*, not a settled fact.** Under Lyon's biology-first criteria a case can be *stated*; deflationary critics read the same data as ordinary adaptive physiology. The evidence licenses "**arguably a candidate for basal cognition**," not "**is cognitive.**"
> - **(e) Sentience / consciousness — BRACKETED as undetermined.** Neither asserted nor denied; the strong pole (Baluška & Reber's CBC) and the strong deflation are both live, and no evidence settles it.
>
> **Bounded philosophical sub-claim (metaphysical):** what would even *count* as "thought" here is **definition-relative.** On a biogenic/functional definition (goal-directed, valenced, integrative, history-sensitive processing), fungi are a **borderline candidate**; on a definition requiring representation, subjective experience, or a unified point of integration, they are **not** shown to qualify. There is **no framework-independent fact** of whether fungi "think" — the honest output is a graded map, not a yes/no.

---

# Claim Type (KOS-0003 §3)
**Interpretive** (grading against the ladder) **+ metaphysical** (the bounded "what counts as thought" sub-claim). Sentience is bracketed, not adjudicated.

---

# Evidence (KOS-0003 §4)
Type: **Empirical (via the descriptive claims) + framework/philosophical**.
- **From the descriptive base:** CLM-0059 (adaptive foraging, High), CLM-0060 (transport, High), CLM-0062 (integration, Moderate) → firmly establish rungs (a)–(b).
- **CLM-0064:** operational memory-like carry-over only → rung (c) at Low; *Physarum* excluded.
- **CLM-0061/0065:** electrical "language" unsupported; strong neural analogy unwarranted → constrain any leap to (d)/(e).
- **SRC-0087 (Lyon):** the biogenic/basal-cognition **criteria** — the instrument that lets rung (d) be *stated* rigorously; Lyon carefully separates cognition (technical) from sentience.
- **SRC-0088 (Baluška & Reber, bracketed pole):** CBC asserts sentience is coterminous with life — steelmanned as the maximal reading, **not adopted**; it makes sentience true-by-definition and so cannot *discriminate*, which is why (e) is bracketed rather than granted.
- **SRC-0089 (Money, steelman):** "fungal mind" opinion — the enthusiast voice, quarantined from grading.

---

# Evidence Evaluation (KOS-0003 §5, 0–5)
- Reliability: 4 for rungs (a)–(b) (rest on the High descriptive claims); 2 for (d) (interpretive, contested); n/a for (e) (bracketed).
- Relevance: 5 — this is the intelligence question.
- Independence: 4 — the deflationary (Blatt/Taiz), measured (Lyon), and enthusiast (Baluška/Money) poles are all represented.
- Quality: 4 for the graded map; the (d) verdict is a defensible interpretive placement, not a measurement.
- Limitations: establishes where the evidence *lands on a stated ladder*; establishes **nothing** about sentience, and does not resolve the definition of cognition.

---

# Source Evaluation
The verdict deliberately rests its *confidence* on the descriptive claims (which even skeptics accept) and treats the cognition frameworks (Lyon) and the strong poles (Baluška, Money) as **interpretive lenses, not confidence-raisers**. This keeps the verdict from inheriting the enthusiasts' priors.

---

# Assumptions (KOS-0003 §10)
- **Ontological/definitional:** "cognition" and "thought" have **no single agreed definition**; the verdict is explicitly framework-relative (biogenic vs. representation-requiring).
- **Epistemological:** absence of evidence for sentience is treated as **undetermined**, not as evidence of absence — hence bracketing, not denial.
- **Cultural:** the "fungal mind" narrative is a motivated, convenient (and marketable) story — named and resisted; the verdict must survive *without* it, and does (rungs a–b stand on deflationary-accepted data).

---

# Reasoning (KOS-0003 §7)
**Interpretive/abductive against a stated framework.** **Risks checked:** *anthropomorphic projection* (the central named risk — "intelligence/mind/thought" graded, never assumed); *motivated reasoning* (the perennial-enthusiast "fungi are conscious" and the marketable "fungal mind" are named and quarantined; the reserved deflationary convenience "it's just chemistry" is also named — the verdict sits between them); *definitional smuggling* (named — refusing to let a chosen definition of "cognition" pre-decide the verdict); *weakest-link discipline* (the verdict's interpretive rungs (c)/(d) are held at Low; the finding may not exceed them for cognition claims).

---

# Confidence (KOS-0003 §8)
- **Rungs (a)–(b): Level 4 (High)** — inherited from the High descriptive claims.
- **Rung (c): Level 2 (Low)** — operational carry-over only.
- **Rung (d) basal cognition *as a framing*: Level 2 (Low)** — statable, contested, not settled.
- **Rung (e) sentience: not graded — BRACKETED (undetermined).**
- **Headline for the cognition verdict (i.e. "are fungi cognitive?") — Level 2 (Low)**, governed by the weakest necessary interpretive link (rungs c–d). No Level 5 anywhere (reserved; and interpretive/cross-domain inference cannot warrant it — KOS-0003 §8 / OPS-0003).

---

# Limitations
- Licenses "fungi are sophisticated adaptive-information-integrating organisms (rungs a–b, High) and at most borderline candidates for basal cognition (Low), with sentience undetermined"; **does NOT** license "fungi are cognitive/conscious/thinking," and **does NOT** deny sentience either.
- The verdict is **framework-relative by construction** — a feature (honesty), not a hedge.

---

# Alternative Interpretations
1. **Strong cognition/sentience (Baluška, Money, Simard-adjacent enthusiasm).** Steelmanned at full strength. Not adopted: rests on definitions that make the conclusion trivial (CBC) or on opinion (Money); no discriminating evidence.
2. **Strong deflation ("mere chemistry; cognition talk is a category error").** Steelmanned (Blatt/Taiz). Not fully adopted: rungs (a)–(b) are real and the basal-cognition *framing* is a legitimate scientific research program, not a nonsense — so (d) is Low, not zero.
3. **Graded map with (e) bracketed (middle).** Adopted — the honest shape of the evidence.
