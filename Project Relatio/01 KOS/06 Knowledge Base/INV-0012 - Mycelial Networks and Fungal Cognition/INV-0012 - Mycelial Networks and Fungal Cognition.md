# 1. Research Question

**Primary:**

> How do mycelial (filamentous fungal) networks grow, transport resources, and signal internally — and, graded against a defined ladder of cognition, what level of information-processing, adaptive behavior, or "intelligence" does the current evidence warrant attributing to them, and what does it not?

**Secondary:**

> To what extent are mycelial networks genuinely analogous to neural networks — structurally, dynamically, and functionally — and where does the analogy break down?

**The cognition ladder (the grading instrument for the intelligence question — a stated framework, not a loaded premise).** Where evidence is claimed to show "intelligence," it is graded against an explicit ladder and the investigation reports where it actually lands:

- **(a) Stimulus–response** — reactive, non-integrated.
- **(b) Adaptive foraging / information integration** — resource-directed growth, reallocation, whole-network coordination.
- **(c) Learning & memory** — habituation, sensitization, anticipation, stored spatial "memory."
- **(d) Minimal / basal cognition** — the technical sense in the basal-cognition literature (goal-directedness, valence, integration over time) — a scientific construct, not a claim of sentience.
- **(e) Sentience / "thought" / consciousness** — **explicitly bracketed as currently undetermined.** The investigation neither asserts nor denies rung (e); it grades where the evidence reaches and states plainly what is beyond current settlement.

> **Register discipline.** "Intelligence," "communication," "memory," and "thought" are **terms under examination, never premises.** The popular "Wood Wide Web" and "fungal mind" framings are treated as claims to be tested, not adopted vocabulary. Both poles — the enthusiast ("fungal cognition") and the deflationary skeptic ("mere chemistry / anthropomorphism") — are **steelmanned at their strongest** (OPS-0003 Non-Negotiable 6). Where the evidence is genuinely split, a **split verdict at honest confidence is the answer** (Non-Negotiable 7).

# 2. Scope & Disambiguation

## 2.1 Subject: filamentous fungi forming mycelial networks
Saprotrophic (wood-decay, cord-forming) and mycorrhizal fungi that grow as hyphal networks. **Excludes** single-celled yeasts (no mycelial network) except where cited for a cellular mechanism.

## 2.2 The load-bearing disambiguation: *Physarum* is NOT a fungus
***Physarum polycephalum* (the acellular slime mold) is a protist (Amoebozoa), not a fungus.** The famous maze-solving and "Tokyo rail network" optimization results are **slime-mold** work and are **constantly mislabelled as fungal** in popular coverage. In this investigation *Physarum* is admissible **only as a clearly-labelled comparandum** for network-level adaptive behavior — **never as evidence about fungi.** Any claim that imports a *Physarum* result as a fungal capacity is a scope error to be flagged.

## 2.3 The neural-network analogy is a claim to test, not a bridge to assume
The secondary question is answered by **evaluating** the analogy along three axes and naming the disanalogies, not by assuming it (KOS-0003 §7 analogical-reasoning check — "are the similarities sufficient to justify the comparison?"):
- **Structural:** nodes/edges, hubs, small-world topology, anastomosis vs. synapses.
- **Dynamical:** propagating electrical potentials vs. action potentials; integration; timescales (seconds–hours vs. milliseconds).
- **Functional:** information processing, plasticity, learning.
Disanalogies (no neurons, no synaptic action potentials in the neuronal sense, no central integrator, orders-of-magnitude slower dynamics) are stated as first-class content, not footnotes.

## 2.4 Excluded by construction
- **No individualized or applied claims** (agriculture, medicine, psychoactive fungi pharmacology) — this is about network biology and cognition, not application.
- **No metaphysics of consciousness beyond the bracket** — rung (e) is bracketed; the investigation does not adjudicate the hard problem.
- **No magnitude ranking** of fungi/behaviors by "how intelligent" absent a stated metric.
- **The historical/philosophical panpsychism debate** is out of scope except as a named, bracketed alternative for the single bounded philosophical claim (§3, claim 8).

## 3.1 Claim-type discipline (the spine of this investigation)
This topic spans the **entire KOS-0003 §3 claim taxonomy**, and each claim must be **explicitly classified** so that a *descriptive* finding (what is observed) is never silently upgraded into a *causal* or *interpretive* one (what it means):
- **Descriptive** (growth, transport, the electrical-spiking *datum*) — graded on directness of observation.
- **Causal** (signaling *coordinates* behavior; foraging is *adaptive*) — requires mechanism + alternatives + confounds.
- **Interpretive** (the neural analogy; where on the cognition ladder) — requires the framework stated and competing interpretations.
- **Metaphysical** (the bounded "what would count as thought here" sub-claim) — assumptions and competing models named; sentience bracketed.

**Operative discipline:** the strongest hazard in this domain is the **datum→interpretation slide** — e.g., "hyphae produce electrical spike trains" (a defensible descriptive datum) silently becoming "fungi have a language" (a contested interpretive claim). Claim 3 must hold these apart. Likewise the **Physarum→fungus import** (§2.2) and **anthropomorphic projection** are named reasoning risks checked on every interpretive claim.

## 3.2 Decomposition (8 claims, CLM-0059…CLM-0066)
1. **Growth & adaptive foraging** — hyphal tip extension; resource-directed growth, thickening of productive cords, regression of unproductive ones. *(descriptive/causal)*
2. **Resource transport** — bidirectional translocation of nutrients/water; source–sink regulation across the network. *(descriptive)*
3. **Electrical signaling** — propagating electrical potential spikes in hyphae/mycelium (the *datum*), held apart from the contested *interpretation* that they encode/transmit information ("language"). *(descriptive datum + flagged interpretive over-reach)*
4. **Chemical / Ca²⁺ signaling & whole-colony integration** — non-electrical coordination; the colony as an integrated unit. *(descriptive/causal)*
5. **Mycorrhizal inter-plant transfer ("Wood Wide Web")** — the live **Simard vs. Karst, Jones & Hoeksema (2023)** dispute over plant-to-plant resource/information transfer via common mycorrhizal networks; both poles steelmanned. *(causal/contested)*
6. **Learning / memory-like behavior** — evidence (and its limits) for habituation, anticipation, or stored spatial "memory" in fungi specifically, with the *Physarum* conflation explicitly flagged. *(causal/interpretive)*
7. **The neural-network analogy** — structural / dynamical / functional, with disanalogies named. *(interpretive/analogical)*
8. **The cognition verdict** — where on the ladder (a–e) the fungal evidence lands (rigorous basal-cognition framework), **plus one bounded philosophical sub-claim** on what would even *count* as "thought" here; **sentience (rung e) bracketed as undetermined.** *(interpretive/metaphysical)*

## 3.3 Watch-item (architectural, not a task)
This is a strongly **empirical, quantitative** investigation (spiking frequencies/amplitudes, translocation fluxes, meta-analytic effect sizes on CMN transfer). **GB-2026-002** (optional quantitative-evidence fields — sample size, effect size, replication count — deferred pending a *third* empirical investigation that strains against their absence) should be assessed at closing: did the prose evidence fields adequately carry the quantitative content, or did their absence bite? Primary synthesis: **[[FND-0012 - Mycelial Network Signaling and the Bounds of Fungal Cognition]]**. Verdict in brief: fungal mycelia are **genuinely sophisticated adaptive networks** (forage, translocate, integrate as whole colonies — **High**), but graded against the §1 ladder the evidence lands at **rungs (a)–(b)** firmly, reaches only **operationally into (c)**, warrants **(d) basal cognition only as a contested framing (Low)**, and leaves **(e) sentience bracketed/undetermined.** The strong "language," "wood-wide web," and "fungal brain" claims are **not supported.** The affirmative network biology is High; the cognitive interpretation of it is Low — an asymmetry that *is* the finding.
