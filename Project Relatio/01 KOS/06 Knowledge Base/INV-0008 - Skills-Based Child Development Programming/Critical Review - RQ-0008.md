# 1. Honest limits (read first)

**1.1 Procedural independence only (ROLE-0004 §5).** Reviewer and Specialist are the **same underlying model**, separately invoked. This catches overclaiming, calibration drift, uncatalogued evidence, arithmetic, and citation error — and did (§§3–8). It **cannot** clear a blind spot the two of us share. Here the shared prior is culturally obvious and named by the Specialist: **"a wholesome, hands-on, mentored, outdoor skills program is good for kids"** is a conclusion both of us, and the surrounding culture, find congenial. §3.6 assesses whether the guard against it actually held.

**1.2 Stakes — HEALTH / HIGH-STAKES CLASS.** This is child-facing developmental programming. Unlike RQ-0007 (non-clinical), the **STD-0006 §7.5 reliance gate binds hard** here: nothing in INV-0008 may inform any client-facing, participant-facing, or external-reliance use until **independently re-verified outside Project Relatio's own circuit** (a different model and/or a qualified human — developmental psychologist / child-program evaluator). The internal adversarial pass is **necessary but not sufficient** for this class. The gate is stated on the INV and FND headers; §10 confirms it and sharpens it.

**1.3 Verification strength — MIXED, moderate.** Live re-verified **this session by the reviewer** (independent of the Specialist's own checks): **DuBois et al. 2011 overall d ≈ 0.21** ("small but significant"; ~nine percentile points; *Psychological Science in the Public Interest*) — **CONFIRMED**; **Multon, Brown & Lent 1991** citation (*Journal of Counseling Psychology* 38:30–38) and its self-efficacy→performance/persistence finding — **CONFIRMED**, and the **r ≈ .38 / ~14% variance** performance figure is the standard, widely-reported value (.38² ≈ .144), so the Specialist's "approximate" flag was appropriately cautious, not a fabrication risk; **Bandura 1997** four-sources hierarchy with **enactive mastery as the strongest source** — **CONFIRMED** (added this session as SRC-0053 in remediation, see §8). **NOT independently re-verified this session:** Brussoni et al. 2015 GRADE specifics, Kaleta et al. 2025 beyond the Specialist's WebFetch, Rosanbalm & Murray 2017, Fiese et al. 2002 pagination, Tierney et al. 1995 SMD 0.06. These are honestly flagged on the objects. **Fabrication check: none found** — every source resolves to a real, correctly-attributed publication.

# 2. What I attacked

Reasoning risks (KOS-0003 §7) across seven claims, one entity, one finding, seven (now eight) sources; the 0–5 grades (§5), with focus on (a) the **underlying Level-4** grades (mastery, mentoring) — do they earn High? and (b) the **application-grade ceiling of Level 1–2** — is it honestly held, or secretly a floor being nudged upward for the domain the program most wants? The **weakest-link cap** on the compound (does the synthesis exceed its weakest necessary leg?); the ecological-transfer assumption (the Specialist's nominated weakest point); the steelmanning of alternatives; whether any **uncatalogued companion** silently carries a claim's headline; and the **motivated-reasoning** direction, checked by tracing each claim's load-bearing wording back to its catalogued source.

# 3. Per-dimension analysis

## 3.1 Evidence grading (KOS-0003 §5)

Mostly defensible and, where weak, **honestly low** — CLM-0039 (family ritual) graded Reliability 2 / Quality 2 and application Level 1 is exemplary candour; CLM-0035 **splitting** the risky-play grade (Moderate for PA/social, Low for the regulation/anxiety strand the field over-sells) is exactly the discipline the domain needs. Two systemic observations:

- **Uncatalogued-companion pattern (the material flag — same class as RQ-0007 CLM-0029/0031).** Two claims lean part of their **headline** on a source that is invoked but not catalogued:
  - **CLM-0036** — the title asserts "**Mastery … Are the Strongest Source of Self-Efficacy**." The one catalogued anchor, **Multon 1991 (SRC-0050), does not establish that** — Multon measures the self-efficacy↔achievement association. The "strongest source" proposition rests entirely on **Bandura, invoked in prose but not catalogued.** That is the claim's own title resting on an uncatalogued source.
  - **CLM-0034** — the claim asserts "reduced **inattention/hyperactivity** symptoms," but the catalogued anchor **Kaleta 2025 (SRC-0047)** supports short-term **attentional functioning**; the hyperactivity-symptom corroboration is attributed to "widely-reported greenspace reviews, **not separately catalogued**."
  Both are **disclosed**, and both correctly cap application confidence low — but per KOS-0003 §5/§12 an uncatalogued source cannot silently carry a claim's headline. **Remediated in §8** (Bandura catalogued as SRC-0053; CLM-0034 narrowed to what Kaleta supports).
- **Single-catalogued-anchor property.** Domains 1, 3, 5, 6 each rest on **one** catalogued source. Each is disclosed and correctly caps confidence; I note it as a property of a deliberately non-exhaustive evidence base (INV §2 says so), not a defect to escalate — but it is why no underlying grade should be read as more than its single anchor licenses.

## 3.2 Confidence calibration (KOS-0003 §8)

**Well-calibrated and appropriately deflationary — the strongest feature of the investigation.**
- **Underlying Level-4** on **CLM-0036** (mastery/self-efficacy) and **CLM-0037** (mentoring) is **earned, not inflated**: both are foundational, heavily-replicated bodies; both are held **below Level 5** for the right reasons (correlational/reciprocal; small and heterogeneous effects). The DuBois **d ≈ 0.21** is verified and the record leads with its *smallness* rather than burying it — model behaviour.
- **The application ceiling of Level 1–2 is honestly held.** The temptation would be to let the best-evidenced domain (mentoring) drift to application Level 3+ because the studied interventions really are structured community programs. The record holds it to **Level 2 reaching toward 3** and states three reasons (model mismatch, small magnitude, implementation-contingency). That is the single most important calibration call in the investigation and it was made **against** the convenient direction.
- **The weakest-link cap on the compound (FND-0008 → Level 2)** is correctly constructed via **CLM-0040**: because no study tests the bundle, ingredient strength cannot lift the compound above Low. No claim or synthesis reaches Level 5. **I apply no downward pressure to the confidence levels themselves.**

## 3.3 Assumptions & bracketing (KOS-0003 §10)

Strong. **Ecological transfer** ("each source studied a *specific* intervention, not the proposed bundle") is named as "the single most contestable move" and repeated on every claim. **Provenance laundering** (clinical/at-risk → universal) is named and guarded in domain 5 (CLM-0038) and on ENT-0006. **Composition fallacy** (parts working ≠ whole works) is named in CLM-0040. The **motivated-reasoning guard** is named at the investigation level. This is thorough and, unusually, the bracketing does not hide the ball.

## 3.4 Steelmanned alternatives

Genuinely engaged, not strawmanned: reverse causation (active kids seek risky play; achievement feeds efficacy), third-variable confounding (functional families both ritualize and raise adjusted kids), domain-specificity of self-efficacy (craft ≠ academic ≠ global), and the pro-program steelman (skills mentoring *should* beat friendship mentoring) — each is conceded as **plausible-but-unproven** and correctly routed into the application-grade ceiling rather than used to inflate a claim. CLM-0040's symmetric refusal of *both* "ingredients justify the compound" **and** "no trial means assume no benefit" is the right epistemic posture.

## 3.5 The application-ceiling stress test (my primary attack)

I pushed hardest on whether the Level 1–2 application ceiling is a genuine finding or a defensive reflex. Verdict: **genuine.** It is anchored to a real structural fact (CLM-0040: no compound trial exists) and it is applied **evenly** — the domain with the strongest underlying evidence and the closest ecological match (mentoring) is *still* held to Level 2–3, and the domain the program would most love to claim (self-regulation / resilience / anxiety reduction) is explicitly identified as the **least-evidenced** strand (CLM-0035 regulation split; CLM-0038 no magnitudes). A motivated synthesis would have done the opposite. The ceiling survives the attack.

## 3.6 Motivated reasoning (the primary brief)

The convenient conclusion — "a wholesome outdoor mentored skills program is good for kids" — is named and, critically, the **compound version of it does not survive**: FND-0008 states in its own headline that the compound is "a reasonable, evidence-informed hypothesis, **not a demonstrated intervention**," and §5's "build-and-evaluate" implication is explicitly gated and labelled "**not an endorsement**." Tracing the load-bearing wording back to sources, I find **no vault-vocabulary leak of the kind that flagged RQ-0007** — the synthesis language ("ingredients," "compound," "build-and-evaluate") is the reviewer's/Specialist's own analytic frame, not an import that is then claimed as an independent finding. The guard **holds** here. The one residual: FND-0008 §5 will feed downstream design work (this is why it exists), so its descriptive-not-prescriptive framing must be **preserved verbatim** if it is ever quoted — see §10.

# 6. Shared-frame caveat (same-model limit)

Beyond the individual flags, Specialist and reviewer may share one disposition this investigation cannot self-clear: a cultural prior that **structured, wholesome, mentored, outdoor activity is developmentally good for children.** §3.5–3.6 show the guard held at the level of the *compound* claim (it is explicitly not demonstrated) and that the application ceiling was applied evenly. But a same-model pass cannot certify it caught every place the prior tinted an *underlying* grade upward. Because this is **health/high-stakes**, that residual is not a footnote — it is the reason the §10 reliance gate is absolute pending outside re-verification.

---

# 7. Overall verdict

**CONFORMANT WITH FLAGS. No Blocked objects. No fabrication.** The investigation is epistemically sound in its bones: the KOS-0003 pipeline is followed; dual grades separate what each literature supports from what transfers to the proposed program; the application ceiling of Level 1–2 is honestly and evenly held; the compound program is explicitly **not** demonstrated; the two hardest numbers verify; and the motivated-reasoning guard holds at the synthesis level. The flags were **two uncatalogued-companion defects** (CLM-0036 Bandura; CLM-0034 hyperactivity strand) and **two minor items** (CLM-0037 grade-wording; one graph reciprocal) — all remediated in §8. This is a **strong** investigation for its class; its honesty about its own weakness (the compound is untested; the wanted outcomes are the least evidenced) is its best feature.
