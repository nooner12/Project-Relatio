# Ziran (自然)

---

# 1. Identification

**Term:** ziran (Pinyin *zìrán*), Chinese 自然, lit. "self-so."
**Domain:** philosophical Daoism; the character of the Dao's own operation.

---

# 2. Definition

*Ziran* means **spontaneous naturalness** — things being and acting "of themselves," without external compulsion or contrivance. It describes the Dao's mode of generation (it produces without intending) and the ideal state of things left to follow their own nature.

---

# 3. Classification (KOS-0004)

- **Entity category (KOS-0004 §6):** Abstract Entity — a concept describing a mode of being/becoming.
- **Reality layer:** Layer 5 (Abstract Reality); descriptive of Layer 1–2 natural processes.

---

# 4. Relationship to Wu Wei

*Ziran* and *wu wei* are complementary: *ziran* is the **spontaneous naturalness** of the world; *wu wei* is the **mode of action** by which an agent aligns with it. One acts *wu wei* so that things may be *ziran*.
